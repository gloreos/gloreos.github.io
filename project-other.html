Stacks

Stacks is your traditional cloud storage platform with a special emphasis on collaboration.

FINAL TAKEAWAYS

Use plain language. Interface copy needs to be clear and simple. I realized I shouldn’t try to impress users in the product. All I need to do is guide them to where they need to go.

User testing is key. The user tests I conducted were immensely helpful for narrowing my scope and making my design more straightforward.

Design takes diligence. With each iteration, I had to go back into my mockups and make several little tweaks. This was both detail-oriented and time-intensive. Design may look like lines and buttons on a page, but there’s a lot that goes into it.

Steps for improvement

Start off with strong user research. Although the user surveys were helpful for providing general guidance, I would have liked to understand my users more personally. Next time, I would devote more time to research and interviewing my users.

Use components or symbols. I didn’t realize the importance of using layers and symbols until I found myself copy and pasting elements over and over again. Next time, I will start my design process by creating a components library and name my layers as I’m going.
