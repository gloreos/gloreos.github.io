// CONSUMER ONBOARDING

Background

The goal of the Consumer Engagement team was to increase conversion and overall usage of our native apps. The team decided to start by redesigning the login and sign up flows, which were long overdue for an update.

Solution

In addition to the generic sign up, there were four additional actions that prompted users to register.

  • Save a home
  • Save a search
  • Request a tour
  • Contact an agent
