// CONSUMER ONBOARDING

Background

The goal of the Consumer Engagement team was to increase conversion and overall usage of our native apps. The team decided to start by redesigning the login and sign up flows, which were long overdue for an update.

Solution

In addition to the generic sign up, there were four additional actions that prompted users to register.

  • Save a home
  • Save a search
  • Request a tour
  • Contact an agent

// SINGLE SIGN-ON

Background

Agents have to juggle plenty of tools; it’s difficult to remember the login credentials for all of them. We decided to consolidate these tools under a single sign-on experience, already provided by our business stakeholders. I wrote copy that helped ease agents to this change.

Solution

  • Z* will be moving to a new login experience. Check it out now.
  • Select your brand.
  • What's new? We've improved Z*'s login experience so you can sign in through your G* portal. Bookmark this page for the future or access Z* from the G* portal itself.
